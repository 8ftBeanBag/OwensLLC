About

Owens Landscaping LLC was founded in 2019 by then 18 year old entrepreneur Gideon Owens. After several years gaining experience at a local landscaping company, he decided to take his skills and talent to the next level by starting his own company. Gideon exemplifies hard work and efficiency while also providing top notch customer service. Despite his young age, his customers find that his work is equal to that of more experienced landscapers. Some of his favorite parts of working on landscaping jobs is utilizing his creativity and working hard to provide satisfaction to his customers. Gideon is originally from Michigan but now calls Alachua, FL his hometown.
