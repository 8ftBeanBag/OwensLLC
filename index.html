About

Owens Landscaping LLC was founded in 2019 by then 18 year old entrepreneur Gideon Owens. After several years gaining experience at a local landscaping company, he decided to take his skills and talent to the next level by starting his own company. Gideon exemplifies hard work and efficiency while also providing top notch customer service. Despite his young age, his customers find that his work is equal to that of more experienced landscapers. Gideon decided to challenge himself by including tree work in his business. In the last couple of years, he has become an experienced tree worker, bringing innovation and creativity to his work while keeping to the highest safety standards.

Gideon is originally from Michigan but now calls Alachua, FL his hometown. As a local business owner he has interfaced with several other businesses. Many have contracted him to work for them due to his integrity and work ethic.

Gideon spends most of his spare time sharpening his chainsaws. When he is done, he enjoys fishing, spending time with friends, and taking road trips. His favorite way to spend an evening is enjoying time with friends and family by a bonfire.
